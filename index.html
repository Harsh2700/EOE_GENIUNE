About EOE Genuine

EOE Genuine is a pioneering startup dedicated to transforming vehicle efficiency through innovative solutions. Our core focus areas include:

  • Advanced Controller Units (ECUs): Developing cutting-edge ECUs to enhance performance and fuel efficiency in both electric and traditional vehicles.
  • Electric Vehicle Conversions: Offering services to convert petrol and diesel vehicles into electric models, promoting sustainable transportation options.
  • Mechanics Training and Workshops: Providing specialized training programs and workshops for mechanics to upskill in the latest automotive technologies.
  • Spare Parts Supply: Supplying essential spare parts, including sensors and batteries, to support vehicle maintenance and conversions.
  • Web Solutions for Automotive Professionals: Delivering tailored web services to enhance the online presence and operational efficiency of automotive businesses.

Established in 2023, EOE Genuine is committed to driving automotive innovation and contributing to a more sustainable future. Connect with us on LinkedIn to stay updated on our latest initiatives and offerings.
